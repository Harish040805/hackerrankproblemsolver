Beautiful Days at the Movies (By Harish)

Instructions

What is this web app about?

This app determines how many "Beautiful Days" exist between the given range. A beautiful day is one where the absolute difference between the day number and its reverse is divisible by a divisor k.

Practical Use of This Concept:

  • Useful in learning digit manipulation.
  • Helps understand divisibility and numerical symmetry.
  • Used in coding tests to assess loop and math logic.

How to use:

  • Enter start day (i).
  • Enter end day (j).
  • Enter divisor (k).
  • Click "Check Beautiful Days".

Input Guidelines:

  • i, j, and k must be integers.

Output:

  • Shows total number of Beautiful Days.
