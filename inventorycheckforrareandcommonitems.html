Instructions

What is this web app about?


This tool evaluates any product or item ID you enter to check two distinct mathematical properties at the same time: if it is a Strong Number and if it is a Prime Number. Based on these checks, it labels the item under one of four unique categories.


🐦‍🔥 Rare Item vs Common Item 🧻:


"🐦‍🔥 Rare Item: A number where the factorials of its separate digits add up to the number itself. For example, with 145, calculating $1! + 4! + 5!$ gives $1 + 24 + 120 = 145$.

🧻 Common Item: Any number where the digit factorials do not add up to the original number.


👑 Premium Item vs Regular Item 📂


👑 Premium Product: A number greater than 1 that cannot be divided evenly by any other number except 1 and itself (like 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, or 17).

📂 Regular Product: Any number that has other divisors, or the number 1.


How to use:


  • Step 1: Enter any whole number greater than or equal to 0 into the input box.
  • Step 2: Click the Analyze Item Status button.
  • Step 3: View your results instantly. The page will show the calculation breakdown and display two color-coded badges indicating the item's exact category combination right below.
